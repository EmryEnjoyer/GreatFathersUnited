Great Fathers United

Building self-reliance

Our Vision


GFU aspires to be one of the leading self-reliance inner city youth programs in Tennessee for young men looking for a safe environment to become an effective leader in the community.

Our Mission


GFU ains to create a safe, secure, self sustainable community for the families of future men.
